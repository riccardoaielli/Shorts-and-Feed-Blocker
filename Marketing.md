# Shorts and Feed Blocker

## Take control of your YouTube experience

Shorts and Feed Blocker helps you eliminate distractions on YouTube so you can focus on what truly matters.

Stop endless scrolling. Clean your feed. Watch with intention.

---

## Features
- Block YouTube Shorts from feed and search  
- Remove recommended videos  
- Hide comments for a cleaner experience  
- Simple toggle controls  
- Fully customizable blocking options  

---

## Why it matters
YouTube is designed to keep you watching longer.  
This extension helps you take that control back.
