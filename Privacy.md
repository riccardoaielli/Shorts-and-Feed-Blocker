# Privacy Policy – Shorts and Feed Blocker

## Overview
Shorts and Feed Blocker is a browser extension designed to improve focus by removing distractions from YouTube, including Shorts, recommendations, and comments.

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

---

## Data Collection
This extension does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

We do not:
- Track your browsing activity  
- Collect personal information  
- Store user analytics  
- Send any data to external servers  

---

## Local Storage
All settings (such as enabled or disabled toggles) are stored locally in your browser only.  
This data never leaves your device.

---

## Third Parties
This extension does not use any third-party analytics, tracking tools, or external APIs.

---

## Permissions
Any permissions used by the extension are strictly required to:
- Modify YouTube page content (e.g., hide Shorts, comments, or recommendations)

They are not used for data collection.

## Changes to This Policy
This privacy policy may be updated in the future. Any changes will be reflected in this document.
